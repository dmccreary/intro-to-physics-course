# Concept Taxonomy

This taxonomy organizes the 200 physics concepts into 12 thematic categories for improved navigation and learning pathway visualization.

## 1. Foundation (FOUND)

**Description:** Core scientific practices, measurement, mathematical tools, and fundamental skills required for all physics study.

**Concepts Include:** Scientific method, measurement, units, significant figures, error analysis, dimensional analysis, vectors, scalars, graphical analysis, mathematical prerequisites.

## 2. Kinematics (KINEM)

**Description:** The study of motion without considering forces - displacement, velocity, acceleration, and their graphical representations.

**Concepts Include:** Linear motion, projectile motion, kinematic equations, motion graphs, free fall, relative velocity.

## 3. Dynamics (DYNAM)

**Description:** The study of forces and their effects on motion, including Newton's Laws and applications.

**Concepts Include:** Force, Newton's Laws, friction, tension, normal force, equilibrium, inclined planes, pulleys, centripetal force.

## 4. Energy (ENRGY)

**Description:** Work, energy transformations, conservation of energy, power, and simple machines.

**Concepts Include:** Work, kinetic energy, potential energy, conservation of energy, power, efficiency, simple machines, mechanical advantage.

## 5. Momentum (MOMNT)

**Description:** Linear momentum, impulse, collisions, and conservation of momentum.

**Concepts Include:** Linear momentum, impulse, elastic and inelastic collisions, center of mass, rocket propulsion.

## 6. Rotation (ROTAT)

**Description:** Rotational motion, torque, angular momentum, and rotational dynamics.

**Concepts Include:** Angular displacement, angular velocity, torque, rotational inertia, angular momentum, rolling motion.

## 7. Oscillations (OSCIL)

**Description:** Periodic motion, simple harmonic motion, springs, pendulums, and resonance.

**Concepts Include:** Simple harmonic motion, amplitude, frequency, period, Hooke's Law, pendulums, damping, resonance.

## 8. Waves (WAVES)

**Description:** Wave properties, behaviors, and phenomena including interference and wave types.

**Concepts Include:** Mechanical waves, transverse and longitudinal waves, wavelength, frequency, interference, standing waves, reflection, refraction, diffraction, Doppler effect.

## 9. Sound (SOUND)

**Description:** Sound waves, acoustic properties, intensity, and sound phenomena.

**Concepts Include:** Sound waves, speed of sound, intensity, decibel scale, pitch, loudness, beats, harmonics, resonance.

## 10. Optics (OPTIC)

**Description:** Light behavior, reflection, refraction, mirrors, lenses, and optical phenomena.

**Concepts Include:** Reflection, refraction, mirrors, lenses, Snell's Law, dispersion, diffraction, polarization, ray diagrams.

## 11. Light (LIGHT)

**Description:** The electromagnetic nature of light, spectrum, and fundamental light properties.

**Concepts Include:** Light waves, electromagnetic spectrum, visible spectrum, speed of light, luminous intensity, color.

## 12. Electricity (ELECT)

**Description:** Electric charge, electric forces, fields, and electric potential.

**Concepts Include:** Electric charge, conductors, insulators, charging methods, Coulomb's Law, electric fields, electric potential, voltage.
